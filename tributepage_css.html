__RO-HIT SHARMA__

Born: 30 April 1987




Rohit Gurunath Sharma

is an Indian International cricketer and the current Captain of India national cricket team in all formats. Widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of this era, Sharma is known for his timing, elegance, six-hitting abilities and leadership skills. He is the record holder for most international sixes across all formats and also the most number sixes in a calendar year. He plays as a right-handed batsman for India national cricket team in international cricket, Mumbai Indians in IPL and for Mumbai in domestic cricket. As a captain, he led Indian national team to reach the final of 2023 Cricket World Cup.

Rohit Sharma also captains Mumbai Indians and the team has won 5 titles in 2013,2015,2017,2019 and 2020 under this leadership, making him the most successful captain in IPL history. Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer who has represented the national team since 2007. He has made 45 centuries in international cricket—31 in One Day Internationals (ODI), 10 in Tests and 4 in Twenty20 Internationals (T20Is)—as of October 2023.
